Over last 3 decades we have established our presence in Secunderabad, India and pride ourselves in our expanding and a strong student Alumni.

Over the past 30 + years our students have successfully secured seats across India's most premier Engineering colleges. Other success stories belong to our students who have been consistently winning global competitions under the guidance and direction of founder Mr. Manjunath and his founding team of teachers.
